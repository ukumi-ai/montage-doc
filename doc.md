# Creating Video Clips with Montage: A Complete Guide

**What You'll Learn**

- How to upload videos and set up your first Montage project
- How to use AI to automatically identify clip-worthy moments in your videos
- How to customize captions with different fonts, styles, and positioning
- How to export and download your finished clips for social media

## Introduction

Montage is an AI-powered video editing platform that helps you transform long-form videos into engaging short clips optimized for social media. Whether you're creating content for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, or LinkedIn, Montage automates the tedious process of finding compelling moments, generating transcripts, and adding professional captions.

This guide walks you through the complete workflow—from uploading your first video to downloading polished, platform-ready clips.

## Configuring AI Clip Generation

Once your video finishes uploading, Montage will automatically transition to the clip configuration screen where you'll define what types of moments to extract.

### Defining Clip-Worthy Moments

1. Wait for the "Upload successful" confirmation message
1. Locate the text box under "What Clip-worthy moments are you looking for in this video?"
1. Type a detailed description of the content you want to extract

For example: "Find interesting clips which have a good hook in the start: 2-3 seconds"

Configure your clip generation settings using the following options:

| Parameter | Description | Options |

## Reviewing and Refining Your Clips

After the AI completes its analysis, you'll see a dashboard displaying all identified clip-worthy moments.

### Understanding the Clip Review Interface

The clip review screen consists of several key elements:

- **Clip selector row** - Blue and gray rectangles representing each suggested clip (currently selected clip appears in blue)
- **Video preview** - Playback of the selected clip
- **Clip details** - Title, description, and relevance scores
- **Platform ratings** - Fit scores for each target platform (e.g., LinkedIn Fit)

### Reviewing Individual Clips

1. Click on any clip rectangle in the top row to select it
1. Watch the video preview by clicking the play button
1. Review the clip's title and AI-generated description
1. Check the prompt relevance score to see how well it matches your criteria
1. Note the platform-specific fit ratings to prioritize clips for your target audience
1. Repeat for each suggested clip to compare options

To fine-tune a clip's content:

1. Click the **Refine script** button for your selected clip
1. Review the automatically generated transcript alongside the video preview
1. Edit the transcript text to improve clarity or remove filler words
1. Use the video preview to verify your edits align with the actual content
1. Save your changes before moving to the next clip

## Customizing Captions

Professional captions improve accessibility and engagement across all social media platforms. Montage provides extensive customization options to match your brand style.

### Accessing Caption Settings

1. Click **Next: Captioning** after completing the reframing process
1. Select the **Styling** tab to access caption customization options
1. Watch the live preview update in real-time as you make changes

### Caption Styling Options

Configure your captions using these settings:

| Setting | Options | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Font** | Inter, Arial, Helvetica, Impact, and more | Set the typeface for your captions |
| **Size** | Small, Medium, Large | Control text size for readability |
| **Style** | Karaoke, Boxed | Choose between word-by-word highlighting or background boxes |
| **Position** | Top, Middle, Bottom | Determine where captions appear on screen |
| **Casing** | Original, UPPERCASE, lowercase, Capitalize | Control text capitalization |
| **Colors** | Custom color picker | Set text and background colors |

![Caption styling interface with font selection dropdown](https://apsqapfbnzjhtsiorvhl.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/sign/production/images/taqei9tj5yul/1685/3bd88ca5-b345-41b3-9d21-84078f4e0937?token=eyJraWQiOiJzdG9yYWdlLXVybC1zaWduaW5nLWtleV83NWYxM2EzMS04OWM2LTRhMDAtODRlMC03OWFiZGUyOWEwMmYiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9.eyJ1cmwiOiJwcm9kdWN0aW9uL2ltYWdlcy90YXFlaTl0ajV5dWwvMTY4NS8zYmQ4OGNhNS1iMzQ1LTQxYjMtOWQyMS04NDA3OGY0ZTA5MzciLCJpYXQiOjE3NjgyMzUzNTAsImV4cCI6MTc2ODI0OTc1MH0.PXvYr8oFtqUgYARdI8gcT9EXEfPheWqj8pBsqIM7yvk)

### Step-by-Step Caption Configuration

1. Click the font dropdown and select your preferred typeface
1. Choose a text size (Small, Medium, or Large) that balances readability with screen space
1. Select a text style—use "Karaoke" for dynamic word-by-word highlighting or "Boxed" for traditional background boxes
1. Pick a position that doesn't obscure important visual content (Bottom is most common for talking-head videos)
1. Set the text casing to match your brand style
1. Use the color picker to set text and background colors for optimal contrast
1. Review the live preview to ensure captions are legible and aesthetically pleasing

**Note:** Caption position is particularly important—avoid placing captions over faces or key visual elements. Test readability on mobile devices by viewing the preview at a smaller size.
